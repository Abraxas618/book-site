FAQ — THE PATH

Is this real?

This archive makes no claim for you — it is a record: every session faithfully preserved from the spoken record, with only light formatting and transcription cleanup — including forecasts that arrived and forecasts that did not. Read the Start Here path, sit with the material, and weigh it for yourself. That has been the community's stance from the first page of the book to the last page of this site.

How does a session happen?

Dr. Kosol Ouch enters a deep trance — often supported by theSource-Coil Device — and Unimetrix 1 speaks through him: "System online… scanning for entity interface." Students ask; the intelligence answers; the session commonly closes with a healing protocol and the words "Be well. Be at peace." Sessions are held as group sessions within the community.

Can I just book a session with Unimetrix 1?

No — and this is the heart of how this community differs from most.A session is earned, not booked. Knowledge here is not taken lightly, and the interface is not entertainment. The path runs through relationship:

  1. Reach the teacher. ContactDr. Kosol Ouch on Facebook — that is the door to the main group.
  2. Join the main group. Meet the community, listen, and learn how it carries itself.
  3. Train in Baramay. Be taught theBaramay technique directly — the foundation of everything here.
  4. Grow the connection. A real student–teacher relationship forms over time. There is mutual knowledge, mutual trust.
  5. Then — a session. When the relationship is ready, you may take part in a session with Unimetrix 1.

Serious students are welcome. Jokers, trolls, and the merely curious-for-sport are not — not out of unkindness, but because this is a school, and the work deserves the same seriousness you would bring to any teacher worth learning from.
