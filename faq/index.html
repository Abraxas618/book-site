FAQ — THE PATH

Is this real?

This archive makes no claim for you — it is a record: every session faithfully preserved from the spoken record, with light formatting and transcription cleanup and rare, documented redactions (see the editorial policy below) — including forecasts that arrived and forecasts that did not. Read the Start Here path, sit with the material, and weigh it for yourself. That has been the community's stance from the first page of the book to the last page of this site.

What exactly does "cleanup" mean? Has anything been changed?

The transcripts are the spoken record with transcription cleanup only: punctuation, spelling, speaker-name normalization, removal of transcription artifacts (timestamps, duplicated fragments), and formatting. Wording is not rewritten, claims are not softened, and forecasts are never edited after the fact.

There is one narrow exception, and this archive discloses it rather than hiding it:public-interest redactions. Where a passage names a real, identifiable group or person in a way that repeats a recognized defamatory pattern, the specific name may be generalized — never silently. Every such change is disclosed on the affected session's page. The complete list to date:
