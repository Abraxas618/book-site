THE PRACTICE

What follows is preserved from the sessions, exactly as taught in them. But know this: in this community, real training passes from teacher to student. These pages are the record — the path is the practice.

The Five Rules

The foundation, given in the sessions as the operating law of reality:

  1. You exist.
  2. You are here now.
  3. You are one with all that is — the is the one, the one is the all.
  4. What you send out comes back to you.
  5. Everything changes — except rules one through four.

The stated goal: "Be immortal and be in health." The Rules are a test — hold anything against them to see whether it conforms to the law of the universe. (Codex entry)

The Four Pillars

Outside the sessions, Kosol has written a framework for what the practice builds toward — four pillars: Money (economic self-sufficiency),Power (wisdom, knowledge, and health), Magical Ability(the quantum ability to walk and interact with higher intelligence and logic), andPopularity — the combined result of the first three, which opens access to every resource and network a student needs.

On the Devices

Kosol describes the Q Devices and the Source-Coil instrument as living instruments, not tools in the ordinary sense: each is activated through the user's own consciousness — "blowing" life into it through unconditional intent — and functions as a kind of co-pilot for connecting to the collective consciousness without interference. His stated rule is firm: the Device is never to be used for personal gain such as gambling — misuse, he states, carries consequence from theGalactic Federation itself. He and two others in the community hold the ability to deactivate a Device remotely if it is misused.

The Baramay invocation

From the April 7, 2020 session — the command-and-confirmation form ofBaramay: focus your intention, breathe it into your palm, and ask: "Baramay, is it done now?" The same form extends to water and food — "Baramay, please change this water into medicine… make it potent" — followed by the breath, "like pressing the Enter key." (source session)

The angel-mirror meditation

From the March 13, 2020 session: close your eyes and look into a mirror in the mind. To your left and right, see two luminous photonic beings — male and female — your celestial family. They can be approached, spoken with, and touched, producing physical sensation. This is taught as a first doorway to direct perception. (source session)

The healing invocation

From the August 30, 2020 session, the form spoken over another:"Baramay, Universe — restore and regenerate this being on these levels: physical, emotional, and mental — now." Followed by: "Return your light body to your physical construct." In the sessions this grows into the fullBaramay Universe healing protocol — the nanite medical teams and the percentage countdown that close nearly every later session. (source session)

Manifested food and water
