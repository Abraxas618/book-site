What's been added, fixed, or changed in the archive, newest first. The same list is available as JSON for scripts and agents that just want to check whether anything is new.

Codex counts across the site are now computed automatically.
  • Added verified original recordings for twenty-five more sessions (December 2020 through December 2025), each oEmbed-checked against YouTube and linked to jump straight to the Unimetrix 1 segment. Verified-video coverage is now 30 of 55 sessions.
  • Added an on-site video player to every sourced session: press play and the recording starts exactly where the Unimetrix 1 segment begins (and stops where it ends) — no more clicking out to YouTube and hunting for a timestamp. The player loads lazily (a thumbnail until you press play), uses YouTube's privacy-enhanced mode, and keeps a watch-on-YouTube link for attribution.
