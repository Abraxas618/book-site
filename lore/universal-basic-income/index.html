Universal Basic Income

'Operation UBI' — the record's forecast of global basic income, swinging between planetary provision and the control mechanism attached to the chip.

Codex entryOperations & Events

Universal Basic Income is one of the record’s most persistent forecasts — eight sessions across 2020–2023, a dedicated session title (October 22, 2020), and its own operation name. In the utopian register, the October 2020 session promises provision: “Universal Basic income will be global for you all, becoming a planetary civilization… to provide all inhabitants of this planet a complete balanced fairness to support life on a basic level.” The next night’s session gives it the operational stamp: “On all country initiated 2020: Operation UBI” — with the pandemic named as “catalyst.”

But the record refuses to leave it benign. By the February 2023 John Toth session, the same program appears in the description of a “totalitarian society, for efficiency control of our citizens, through universal basic income and quantum brain chip” — UBI as the stipend side of the Neuro-Net bargain, the payment that comes with the interface.

That double vision is the entry’s real content: the record forecasts the same instrument twice — once as the floor under a post-scarcity civilization, once as the leash of a monitored one — and, characteristically, insists the difference is not the technology but the timeline.
