The Arcturians

Nara, physicist of the star nation — teacher of thought-powered craft and consciousness engineering.

The Arcturians speak in the April 14, 2024 session through Nara — “greeting from Arcturian star constellation of Eridanus… I am Nara, physicist of our star nation” — and her subject is the deepest engineering the archive records: technology run on thought.

Nara’s teaching (see Alien Power Sources) reframes what a machine is. Arcturian craft have no engines; they are molecular structures tuned to consciousness, because electricity “cannot traverse interdimensional wormholes” and electromagnetic pollution “hinders your progression to higher vibration.” Civilizations, she says, all pass through the electromagnetic age — and then graduate beyond it, “entering consciousness itself.” Her design canon is geometry: the Fibonacci angle, the universe as resonator; her physics closes in a credo — “Once you believe, therefore, it is real.”

The Arcturians match their reputation in the wider contact literature — scientists and healers of high refinement — and in the archive they share their session with the Dolphin Race’s Specialist Ra, the two voices together turning a single transcript into the collection’s engineering seminar: one teaching the craft, the other the consciousness that powers it.
