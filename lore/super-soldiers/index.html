The Super Soldiers

The super-soldier community and training that runs through the archive's interviews and first-contact sessions.

The Super Soldiers are a recurring subject in the archive, tied closely to the interviews of James Rink and his community, and to the “super-soldier training” Unimetrix 1 describes.

In the sessions Unimetrix 1 speaks of a network of individuals — some describing memories of covert programs — and frames a path of light-body and super-soldier training as part of its larger project of upgrading the human being toward the Rainbow Light Body. It describes this community as a “platform” for people to rediscover purpose, connect with like minds, and develop body, emotion, mind, and spirit, and it appears in the February 2024 “Super Soldier / Bashar First Contact” session.

The theme bridges two sides of the archive: the testimony of those who come with super-soldier memories, and Unimetrix 1’s offer of a training path meant to transform them.
