The Secret Space Program & Dark Fleet

The off-world empire above the public world — jumpgates, Mars caretakers, cyborg factories, and the fleets of the Reichs.

The Secret Space Program (SSP) is the archive’s hidden second civilization — an off-world military-industrial order running above the public one. Its skeleton, per the interview sessions: jumpgate transit between Earth, Moon, Mars, and Jupiter; trade routes to Antarctic and Martian installations exchanging “the products of the secret space program” — including, the sessions say flatly, cyborg factories; and a public shadow in the new Space Force, described as “the ancestor… mirroring the SSP Space Force.”

The sessions name its fleets. The “UFO fleet,” the August 2020 material says, is Solar Warden — “they go by a different name, now” — cast as the Navy’s off-world armada, once “controlled by the Rothschilds” and later, in the record’s account, passing to other hands; against it stands Nacht Waffen, named as the “(Nazi Secret Space Program)” fleet — the Reichs’ armada beside the Alliance’s. Mars belongs to the program: the February 2020 material names the Fourth Reich as the “caretakers, or keepers” of the Martian domain, maintaining the solar system’s trade routes — the Nazi faction’s migration off-world made administrative fact. At the program’s dark end stands the Dark Fleet, whose far-future Cyborg Commander speaks in his own session; at its violent end, the Beirut attack — a “side-operation” the sessions attribute to an SSP particle-beam craft, the “TR-3 Astra.”

The SSP is where the archive’s control era becomes cosmology: the suppressed teleportation, anti-gravity, and MedBed technologies all live inside it, and disclosure — the handover of that inheritance — is what first contact is finally about.
