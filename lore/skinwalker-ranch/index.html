Skinwalker Ranch & the Portal Triangles

The Utah ranch as one grid-point in a planetary polyhedron of portals — the Dragon, Seattle, Alberta, Antarctica, and Arctic Triangles.

Asked about Skinwalker Ranch in the John Toth session of February 9, 2023, Unimetrix 1 gives the famous Utah property a precise place in the archive’s geometry: it is “an interdimensional portal doorway” — a grid point that “allows the interaction of high cosmic culture in the related geographic gravitational area… the patrol and the interaction of cosmic culture, transit and exit of their personnel and quantum displacement devices.” Not a haunted ranch, in this telling, but a border crossing.

And not alone. The session names the wider network in one breath: “the Dragon Triangle, Seattle Triangle, Alberta Triangle, Antarctica Triangle, Arctic Triangle — follow the grid point of the Icosidodecahedron geometric platonic figure.” The Earth, in the record’s physics, is caged in a polyhedron of such doorways — the same ley-line geometry that runs through its account of the Bermuda Triangle (whose entry maps the same idea in the Atlantic), the temple-anchored stargates, and the Platonic-solid architecture claimed for Unimetrix’s own quantum computers.

One session, one answer — but it knots a modern paranormal landmark into the archive’s oldest pattern: geometry as infrastructure, and certain places on Earth as doors that were never fully closed. Preserved as spoken.
