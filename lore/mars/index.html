Mars & the Solar-System Colonies

The inhabited solar system of the record — Mars colonies human and otherwise, Bigfoot's blue tribe, and the settlement map from the Moon to Saturn.

Codex entryPlaces & Phenomena

Mars appears in eleven sessions as the record’s second address: not a dead planet but a neighborhood. The colony map is stated plainly in the August 10, 2020 interview: “And the Colonies of Mars, of the Moon, and Saturn. And other Asteroids throughout the Solar System, with Colonies from Earth” — the secret space program’s settled solar system, running parallel to the public one.

Its residents are not all human, and not all expected. The Bigfoot teacher of the May 2020 session claims it for his own people: “The blue tribe, they are Space race, which is my tribe. We live on the Moon and we live on Mars.” By the December 2025 session the roster has widened into the record’s routine cosmopolitanism: “interacting with other extraterrestrial colony throughout your planet. Moon, Mars, Venus.”

Where the Moon got the record’s infrastructure (the Lunar Operations Command), Mars gets its settlement claims — colonies, jumpgate connections, and populations layered over eras. In the record’s solar system, the red planet’s emptiness is the cover story.
