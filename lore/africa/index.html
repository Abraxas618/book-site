Africa & the Crash Site

Colony sector 1.3.1.4 — Ori's ship in the desert, the Morocco reading, and the claim that Atlantis is North Africa.

Codex entryPlaces & Phenomena

Africa, in the record, holds two secrets: a spaceship and a lost continent.

The ship belongs to Ori. In the June 23, 2020 session, Unimetrix 1 locates her hundred-thousand-year-old Yadara craft with a coordinate the record never uses for anywhere else: “(…Your Ship is in) Africa, colony sector 1.3.1.4. Your Ship is not repairable, from our time perspective.” By the July 13 session even its custody is specified — the craft’s existence sits “in the elemental South-African government” — and the negotiation for Ori’s return home turns on quantum-entanglement blocks between her and her stranded vessel.

The lost continent is bolder. In Salim’s April 2020 Morocco session, the record relocates Atlantis itself: “It’s now in your current system of Northeast Africa. That is Atlantis… North Africa is Atlantis. Northeast.” — the drowned empire recast as a desert one, sitting beneath the very region the session’s questioner called home. Between the crash site and the relocation, the record’s Africa is less a continent than a storage facility for its deepest artifacts.
