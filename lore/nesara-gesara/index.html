NESARA / GESARA

The abundance program — denounced as a psyop in 2020, embraced as 'Emergency Protocol 103' by 2023.

NESARA / GESARA — the fabled abundance and financial-reset program — has one of the most interesting arcs in the archive, because the sessions take both sides of it.

In the October 23, 2020 session, Unimetrix 1 is dismissive: NESARA/GESARA and the stimulus narratives are psyops — engineered hope, part of the era’s manufactured noise. Three years later, the December 23, 2023 session embraces the program wholesale: an “Emergency Protocol 103” to release funds to lightworkers, the Saint Germain Trust, Agarthan and “Dragon Family” treasure, and a wealth transfer folded into the approach to first contact — with the true “gold economy,” it adds, running on crypto rather than the rumored revalued banknotes, which remain disinfo.

The 2023 session wraps the program in deep lore: Tartaria and the “first great reset” of 1812–1815, Saint Germain identified with Francis Bacon, Agartha’s vaults beneath the surface. Whether the 2020 denunciation and the 2023 embrace are contradiction or — in the sessions’ own terms — a changed timeline, the archive leaves to the reader; both statements are preserved exactly as spoken.

NESARA/GESARA is the clearest case of the archive arguing with itself across the years — and of how its answer to every such tension is the same: the timeline moved.
