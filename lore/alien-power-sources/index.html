Alien Power Sources

Craft run by thought — why advanced civilizations abandon electricity, per the Arcturian physicist Nara.

Alien power sources get their fullest treatment in the April 14, 2024 session, where an Arcturian physicist called Nara explains the engineering of her people’s craft — and the answer is startlingly simple: there is no engine. “Basically a structure. No machine, no engine… vibrationally sensitive, reactive to the way you think… run by thought. Thought is the power source of our technological marvel.” The vessel is designed molecularly to align to consciousness itself.

Why not electricity? Nara’s physics is precise: electromagnetic systems “cannot traverse certain interdimensional wormholes or time traveling — it would interfere” — and their pollution “affects your genetic code, hindering your progression to higher vibration.” Every cosmic civilization, she says, passes through an electromagnetic age and then leaves it, “entering consciousness itself.” The design language given is geometric — build “according to this geometric angle… you call Fibonacci,” using the universe itself as resonant source — and the operating principle is the archive’s whole metaphysics in one line: “Once you believe, therefore, it is real.”

Earthly power gets its interim roadmap elsewhere — the “graphene-rod ionic receiver” systems and fusion generators of the DARPA-era sessions — but the Arcturian teaching marks the destination: past anti-gravity, past the grid, to machines that move because a mind asks them to.
