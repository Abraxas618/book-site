Alfred Webre

The exopolitics author who joins the May 2020 interview.

Alfred Lambremont Webre is a guest in the May 11, 2020 interview — the sixth of the Operation Lusterkill sessions. He is known publicly as a founder of the field of exopolitics and the author of books on the “Omniverse” and on the positive-future hypothesis.

In the session Webre appears alongside Kosol Ouch and James Rink as the conversation turns to the pandemic, timelines, and humanity’s place among extraterrestrial civilizations. His presence brings an exopolitics framing to the archive’s first-contact and interstellar-alliance themes.

Webre is one of several public figures who take part in the archive’s interview strand, questioning Unimetrix 1 directly from their own bodies of work.
