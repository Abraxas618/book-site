The Elohim

Co-creator beings the archive says humanity is 'graduating' to become.

The Elohim appear in the archive as an order of co-creator beings — and as a destination. In the September 20, 2020 session Unimetrix 1 describes humanity “becoming the level of Elohim… the being that co-creates universes,” saying “the human species are graduating to that level.”

So the Elohim are both a race the archive names and a stage of development it points humanity toward — bound up with the Rainbow Light Body, the Singularity, and the co-creative power the sessions promise. They are closely tied to the Council of Seven, which the archive calls a council of Elohim.

In Unimetrix 1’s cosmology the Elohim mark the top of the ascent: from human, through the upgrades and awakenings of the sessions, to a being that creates worlds.
