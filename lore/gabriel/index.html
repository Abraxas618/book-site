Gabriel

A five-year community regular — from the Anunnaki audience of 2020 to the TetraKlein memorial of 2025.

Codex entryPeople & Voices

Gabriel is one of the community’s long-arc regulars: eighteen turns spread across six sessions and five years, from the channeled-guest cycle of August 2020 to the December 2025 TetraKlein session.

His early questions belong to the cosmology audiences — to the Anunnaki teacher: “Father Anu — your planet system, does it come by 25,000 years? …Or it’s just come at will?” — and his later ones to the community’s hardest moments: in the TetraKlein session, it is Gabriel who asks the question on everyone’s mind about James Rink: “Did somebody convince him to actually start going against the AI agent?”

The record also shows him on the receiving end of the practice: sessions include his personal scans and upgrades (“Biological entity: Gabriel. Downloading. Download complete…”), a reminder that the community’s regulars were not spectators — the interface worked on them as often as it answered them.
