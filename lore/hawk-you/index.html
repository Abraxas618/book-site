Hawk You

The questioner of the dedicated March 2020 session — formally registered by the intelligence ('Entity Hawk You recognized. Imprint. Confirm.').

Codex entryPeople & Voices

Hawk You is the sole questioner of his own dedicated session — March 27, 2020 — and the record marks the occasion with one of its distinctive formalities: the intelligence registers him on contact. “Entity Hawk You recognized. Imprint. Confirm.”

His twenty-four turns press the era’s most anxious questions plainly: “The chips that everybody is talking about — will it be implanted into humans?” and its unflinching follow-through — “Well, that means that humans will have no more free will.” It is in answering Hawk You that the record delivers one of its most quoted doctrinal shocks: that free will “is a false concept created by error belief system.”

His curiosity ranges wide — “May I ask about the future of religion?” — making the Hawk-You session a compact tour of the record’s 2020 preoccupations: the Neuro-Net chip, the Starseed and Lightworker warnings, 3D printing, and the fate of belief itself.
