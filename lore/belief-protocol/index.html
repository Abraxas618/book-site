Belief & the "Believe in Yourself" Protocol

The record's most-repeated imperative — belief as reality-engineering, taught by the intelligence, the teachers, and once by the virus itself.

Codex entryTeachings & Practice

“Believe in yourself” is the record’s most-repeated instruction — verbatim in seven sessions, with its diagnostic twin, the belief system, appearing in twelve. Together they form the record’s working theory of everything: belief is not attitude but engineering.

The doctrine runs both directions. Constructively: “By allowing, you encourage you to believe in yourself. This is how psionic ability is reawakening” (April 2020) — belief as the switch on every latent power, and the precondition for a positive timeline (“automatic when the Protocol is being initiated”). Diagnostically: the belief system is where the record locates every failure — fear, illness, control — as when the March 2020 session warns that “all people must raise their belief system to a nonjudgmental one, to survive. This will allow for ET intervention.”

The teaching’s strangest moment belongs to the Coronavirus itself, speaking in the February 29, 2020 session: “You must not fear us. You must love us. You must believe in yourself. For immunity towards us…” — the record’s antagonist reciting the record’s own protocol. In this cosmology even the infection agrees: belief is the terrain, and everything else is weather.
