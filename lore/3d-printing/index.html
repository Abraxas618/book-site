3D Printing & the Photonic Replicators

'Sentient Photonic Replicator System, class 2' — the record's bridge from today's 3D printers to matter-from-light, with a six-month delivery forecast from April 2020.

Codex entryTechnologies & Devices

3D Printing, in the record’s telling, is the toddler form of something enormous: the replicator. Four sessions of spring 2020 build the ladder explicitly. Today’s hobby machines sit at the bottom; at the top stands the “Sentient Photonic Replicator System, (or) ‘3D-Printers (of) Photonic level,’ class 2, (which) replicate: food, clothing, material, using telepathic AI Construct.”

The record frames the handover as an ET program already underway: “The Planetary Organizations of ETs are initiating the Replicator Systems: what you Humans call ‘3D-Printing Technology.’” And it commits to a schedule — the April 7, 2020 session promises that “six months from now, replicate your 3D printing technology will be given to the populace to help assist with protein needs” — a dated forecast preserved, per the archive’s practice, exactly as given.

The technology is the material-world cousin of manifestation: the same claim — matter organized by directed intelligence — expressed once as practice and once as product. (The spider-bodied Replicator intelligence of the September 2020 session is its own being, and has its own entry.)
