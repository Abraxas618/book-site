The Starseed Psyop

The first question in the archive — Unimetrix 1's claim that the 'starseed' narrative was a disempowerment operation.

The Starseed Psyop is the subject of the very first question in the archive’s opening session (January 1, 2021), and it sets the tone for much that follows.

Asked for “a 360 on the star seeds,” Unimetrix 1 — and a voice it channels called “Professor Newson” — answers that the entire starseed genre is a psychological operation: a narrative created, in its telling, “to control the generation of your time, to make them perceive impractical knowledge, keep them confused, keep them disempowered.” It attributes the operation not to DARPA but to a Nazi intelligence network, describing its purpose as producing a “non-competition, subordinate” generation easy to control.

As a remedy, Unimetrix 1 points to exactly the tools the rest of the archive develops: accurate scientific and spiritual knowledge, the Five Rules, and the practice that leads to self-empowerment. The Starseed Psyop is, in effect, the problem the whole project sets out to answer.
