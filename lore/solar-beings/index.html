The Solar Beings & the Morphogenetic Field

'The Solar Beings are what you will become' — the Sun as an upgrade mechanism, acting on the body-field the record calls morphogenetic.

Codex entryConcepts

Asked what the Solar Beings are, the April 23, 2020 session answers with the archive’s whole physics of transformation in two sentences: “The Solar Beings are what you will become, in this present timeline, as the acceleration of the directed energy from your Sun raises the consciousness that you have of your morphogenetic field, which in turn will raise your consciousness. Your molecular structure will change. You all will become Light Beings of higher dimensions, as time progresses.”

The morphogenetic field is the load-bearing idea — named in ten sessions, it is the record’s term for the energetic template around the body that solar radiation is said to act upon. It is the field the Upgrade tunes, the field DNA activation rewrites, and the field the 2026 session says the solar flash will drive “to 1,000%” — awakening chakras and unifying, in its words, the multidimensional self.

The destination is the archive’s oldest promise wearing a new name: the Solar Being is the Rainbow Light Body reached by sunlight rather than discipline — humanity photosynthesizing its way back to what the Fall is said to have cost it. The Sun, in this record, is not weather. It is the Upgrade’s delivery system — claimed, dated, and preserved as spoken.
