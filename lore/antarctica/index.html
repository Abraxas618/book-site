Antarctica

The southern continent the archive ties to a hidden Nazi–extraterrestrial base.

Codex entryPlaces & Phenomena

Antarctica appears in the archive as the site of a hidden base linking human and extraterrestrial powers. In the February 29, 2020 interview Unimetrix 1 speaks of a “Base International: extraterrestrial, human, dominated by the Nazi civilisation,” describing how “Nazi Germany established a connection with an ET civilisation” in the far south.

Antarctica is a recurring location in secret-space-program and disclosure narratives, and the archive draws on that backdrop — connecting the continent to the Secret Space Program, the Reichs, and the concealed history it says runs beneath public events.

As a place, Antarctica anchors the archive’s claim that the map of power is not the visible one — that some of the twentieth century’s most consequential arrangements were made at the edges of the world.
