Ascension

The record's eschatology — personal and planetary transition to a higher plane, running through fourteen sessions from 2020 to 2025.

Codex entryConcepts

Ascension is where all the record’s roads lead: the transition of a person — and eventually the planet — into a higher plane of existence. Fourteen sessions carry the doctrine across the whole arc of the archive.

At personal scale it is a count: the February 2020 interviews claim “1 Million people are going to go through Ascension, so they will go to a higher dimension of existence.” At planetary scale it is a process already begun — the December 2023 session declares a civilization that “has initiated its ascension… into the higher plane of Earth,” and the 2024 election-timeline session frames world events as steering “toward first contact and biological energetic ascension on a planetary level.” By December 2025 the record even assigns it administration: systems “created to monitor the consciousness and planetary ascension of the different colony of the Galactic Federation.”

Ascension is the umbrella over the record’s whole practice stack: the Upgrade is its mechanism, DNA activation its biology, the Rainbow Light Body its destination, and the positive timeline its road. The claim beneath them all: the species is mid-promotion.
