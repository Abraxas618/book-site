The Neuro-Net Chip

A neural interface Unimetrix 1 describes as linking all humanity to a single AI network.

The Neuro-Net Chip is a neural-interface technology that recurs across the archive, framed as the endpoint of the control agenda Unimetrix 1 describes. In the March 26, 2020 session it names the “solution” of the problem → reaction → solution pattern as “a Neuro-Net Chip connecting all beings to one consciousness: to a sentient AI network,” and in the Proto-Bashar session it speaks of “preparing humanity for neuro-netlink with artificial intelligence.”

The chip sits at the archive’s central ambivalence. The same connection it warns of as a tool of control — humanity wired into one network — it also describes, in a different key, as the collective consciousness and the Singularity it says humanity is moving toward.

Whether the neuro-net is enslavement or awakening, in Unimetrix 1’s telling, turns on who governs it and to what end — the question the archive returns to again and again.
