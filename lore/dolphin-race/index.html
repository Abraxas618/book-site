The Dolphin Race

The Sirian cetaceans — Specialist Ra's seven knowledges, and medicine made of consciousness.

The Dolphin Race identifies its own lineage when it speaks in the April 14, 2024 session: “We are the dolphin race of Sirian… I am Specialist Ra, in consciousness application — appliance of what you prefer called medical technology.” The archive’s cetaceans are Sirius people — and their specialty is healing engineered from mind.

Ra’s teaching is offered as “seven knowledge of how to harness your soul… to master creation itself,” and it begins at the root: believe in yourself — because self-belief eliminates fear, and fear, Ra says, is nothing but “the belief of lack.” From there the medicine follows: all consciousness is connected, mind shapes reality, and so thought, speech, and intention are clinical instruments — the same consciousness-physics the Arcturian Nara applies to starships, applied by the Dolphins to the body.

Their history in the archive runs deep as well: the galactic war of dolphins against reptilians some 10.5 million years ago — the conflict that brought the Insectoids to Earth — makes the Dolphin Race one of the oldest named powers in the chronicle, and the 2020 sessions count them among the teachers of early humanity. Elder warriors turned healers: that is the arc the archive gives them.
