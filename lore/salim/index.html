Salim

The questioner from Morocco whose dedicated April 2020 session is the record's one international one-on-one audience with Unimetrix 1.

Codex entryPeople & Voices

Salim holds a distinction no other voice in the record shares: a full dedicated session as the sole questioner — the April 7, 2020 session on Morocco’s future, with 48 turns of dialogue. Unimetrix 1 registers him formally on arrival: “One entity detected: Salim from Morocco, North Africa. Greeting Salim.”

His opening is among the record’s most touching statements of what the community sought in the interface: “I’m very honored to meet you and have… the opportunity to learn from someone from the future.” And his central question — “I wanted to ask about the future of Morocco. Do you know how this country is going to be in the future?” — makes his session the record’s only national reading: post-Covid powers, Africa’s place in the coming order, and the Baramay protocol taught to him directly, including his observation that has since traveled through the community: “The crystals are very telepathic.”
