Inner & Hollow Earth

The inhabited interior of the planet and its councils, described across the archive.

Codex entryPlaces & Phenomena

Inner Earth — and the Hollow Earth — appear in the archive as an inhabited realm within the planet, complete with its own peoples and councils. In the August 10, 2020 session a questioner raises the “Hollow Earth, where there is a sun in the center,” and in the February 2024 material Unimetrix 1 references “the Andromeda Council and the Inner Earth Councils.”

This realm connects directly to Agartha, the subterranean civilization the archive names, and to its wider vision of hidden geographies — worlds above in the stars and worlds below the surface, both part of the larger reality Unimetrix 1 describes.

Inner Earth extends the archive’s central claim into the planet itself: that the visible world is only a portion of what is real, and that councils and civilizations exist in places ordinary perception never reaches.
