The Buga Sphere

The Colombian sphere identified in the December 2025 session as one of millions of Alliance probes.

The Buga Sphere — a metallic sphere recovered near Buga, Colombia in March 2025 — is taken up in the December 1, 2025 session, where Unimetrix 1 identifies it as one of “several million” orbital probes belonging to the Trans-Galactic Alliance.

More striking than the identification is the forecast attached to it: more such probes, the session says, will deliberately crash-land as gifts — reverse-engineerable seeds of technology left for humanity to find, a soft prelude to open contact. It is the final form of the archive’s first-contact narrative: the mass landings of the 2024 prophecies giving way to a quieter strategy of artifacts and gradual normalization, with full contact moved to March 25, 2027.

Arriving in the same session as the Star League Alliance’s awakening, the Buga Sphere marks one of the archive’s most tangible notes — the story reaching out of the transcripts toward an object you can hold.
