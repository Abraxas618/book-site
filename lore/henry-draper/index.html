Henry Draper

Host of the August 2020 'A Perspective From the Future' interview — the session that named the Quantum Mirror — and a voice in the Replicator and book-drive sessions.

Codex entryPeople & Voices

Henry Draper hosts one of the record’s pivotal sessions — the August 10, 2020 interview titled “UniMetrix1, A Perspective From the Future” — and returns in the book-drive training and the Replicator session that September, 43 turns in all.

His introduction sets the tone of respectful curiosity the session keeps throughout: “We have a special guest today: Kosol Ouch. Kosol has spent a long time in the future, and he is back here with us now, sharing his wonderful Q-Devices.” His questions dig at the record’s vocabulary — “Can you explain to people what you mean when you refer to ‘side-operations’?” — and it is in his session that the channeling device itself is redefined as a Quantum Mirror, “designed to reflect your Completion back to you.”

The interview he hosted is among the record’s most-cited: the Solar Warden material, the clock-variance that dates the future two years differently, and the Mars and Moon colony accounts all pass through Henry Draper’s patient questioning.
