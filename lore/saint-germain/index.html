Saint Germain & the Trust

The ascended master the record credits with the abundance program — and the trust said to open twice a year, on Christmas and Easter.

Codex entryPeople & Voices

Saint Germain enters the archive as the author of its economics. In the December 23, 2023 session, NESARA is explained as “an abundant program that was created by the ascended being called Saint Germain,” working — the record says — in concert with beings of “extraterrestrial origin, extradimensional origin called Archangel Michael, Archangel Gabriel, Archangel Raphael.” The project is given a start date: around 1700 A.D., when Saint Germain “was initiated to help create the structure of abundance… to separate the two civilizations.”

Attached to the figure is the Saint Germain Trust — the wealth fund the sessions discuss as opening twice a year, on Christmas Day and Easter, whose long-promised release to humanity is folded into the approach to first contact. The 2023 session ties the whole complex together: the Trust, the Agarthan vaults, and the “Dragon Family” treasure as the funding of the coming Golden Age.

The April 2020 interviews add a stranger note: Saint Germain as an enabler of time travel — “Saint Germaine allow this entity to travel back and forth in your time stream,” with Unimetrix 1 claiming its own “drones and temporal agents” documented his activity across history. An ascended master, a banker of the millennium, and a chrononaut’s patron: the record’s Saint Germain is all three at once, preserved as spoken.
