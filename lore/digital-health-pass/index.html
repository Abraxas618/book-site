The Digital Health Pass

The digital identity and vaccine-passport system Unimetrix 1 discusses as a tool of trust and control.

The Digital Health Pass — a digital identity and vaccine-passport system — is discussed by Unimetrix 1 in the January 1, 2021 session. Asked about its purpose, it frames the pass as a system to “create trust” and efficiency between people, technology, and government, tied to mandatory vaccination and a “digital imprint… carried in your bloodstream.”

But the same passage turns stark: Unimetrix 1 states that “those who work against the system will be… routed to FEMA and terminated for disobedience.” It is one of the archive’s most direct and controversial statements, sitting at the intersection of its Operation Lusterkill material and the problem → reaction → solution model of control.

The Digital Health Pass is recorded here exactly as spoken — a stark example of the archive’s forecasts about digital identity, and one to weigh carefully.
