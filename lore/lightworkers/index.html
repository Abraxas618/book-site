The Lightworkers

The named class of incarnated agents — targeted for elimination in the dark-timeline warnings, first in line for the funds in the bright ones.

Codex entryConcepts

The Lightworkers are a named population in the record’s drama — incarnated agents of the light, always listed alongside the Starseeds — and their fortunes track exactly which timeline a session is describing.

In the dark-timeline warnings of spring 2020 they are targets: “Starseeds and Lightworker beings will be eliminated. Only those conforming to the Directive…” — and, in the Hawk You session’s grimmest line, “All starseed lightworkers will be assimilated. Freewill will be terminated.” In the bright-timeline material they are payees: the December 2023 NESARA session orders that the “fund must be released to all starseed light worker and supersoldier operative.”

The same class, slated for elimination in one branch and back-pay in another, is the record’s timeline doctrine made personal: who the lightworkers are never changes — only which future gets them.
