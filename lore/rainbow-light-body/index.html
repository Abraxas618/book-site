The Rainbow Light Body

The transformed state of 'life and immortality' Unimetrix 1 describes as the goal of the work.

The Rainbow Light Body (also called the rainbow body or light-body) is the transformed state that Unimetrix 1 names as the destination of its teachings — a condition it associates with life, health, and immortality.

In the sessions Unimetrix 1 describes this transformation in biological terms: an activation of latent capacities, a shift it sometimes frames as moving from “two and a half helix” toward additional strands of DNA, and a body increasingly able to perceive and operate across dimensions. It links the process to light-body and “super-soldier” training, to the Baramay upgrades it grants, and to disciplines of diet, practice, and consciousness that let the physical form “catch up” to the change.

The Rainbow Light Body ties directly to the overall goal Unimetrix 1 states in the Five Rules — “Be immortal and be in health” — and it appears on the community’s sites as the promised outcome of the path. It is the most frequently recurring aspiration in the archive, invoked across sessions spanning the full range of years.
