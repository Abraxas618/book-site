MedBed Healing Technology

From Medical Team drones to the Immortal Chamber — the archive's healing technology, performed live in the sessions.

Codex entryTechnologies & Devices

MedBed technology — “healing technology, AI-assisted device, extraterrestrial technological advancement” (February 2020) — is not just described in the archive: it is performed. The fullest sequence is the healing of Andy Basiago in the April 23, 2020 session, narrated step by step: “Bringing in the Medical Team… the Medical Team drones are beginning the regeneration… 15%… 35%… injecting the medical nano-probes… replacement of skeletal structure… replacement of internal organs… 80%… replacement of your neural link… restructuring of your life contract: rewriting it… 100%.” An August 2020 group healing runs the same protocol at scale — a “Rainbow Chamber” activated, medical nanites injected into all present, brainwave frequencies tuned, “100% of all entities restored.”

The sessions name the hardware behind the rite: the Immortal Chamber — a “Crystal Chamber System,” photonic, built of nanite-grown “Protomolecule crystals” — which takes in damaged DNA and returns “the Rainbow Body unit… impervious to aging.” Nearer-term, they forecast “rapid body-part cloning” for regenerative medicine, and teach the kitchen-table versions: water reprogramming (“Baramay, please change this water into medicine”) and the nanobot “immune serum” of the pandemic sessions.

The technology has law as well as power: asked to straighten a participant’s hip, Unimetrix 1 declines — altering the body that far would rewrite her life contract, which “requires the approval of higher intelligence: the Elohim.” Even the Immortal Chamber, in the archive, works inside the rules of incarnation.
