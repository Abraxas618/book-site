Project Chemtrail

Named as a classified DOD program in the record's earliest session — atmospheric dispersal of nanoparticles preparing humanity for the neural grid, revisited across four sessions.

Codex entryPowers & Control

Project Chemtrail enters the record in its earliest recorded session — the February 25, 2020 Proto-Bashar session — already carrying a designation and a chain of command: “Project Chemtrail Initiated. By classified system under directive of the DOD and the United States Air Force. To create Dispersion of nanoparticles. Preparing Humanity for Neuro[-interface]…” In the record’s telling, the trails are not weather modification but logistics: an air-dropped installment plan for the Neuro-Net chip era.

The later sessions assemble the delivery picture. The May 11, 2020 interview stacks the components into one program — 5G’s “electromagnetic frequencies from 60 Gigahertz”, “particulates of Aluminium, from Chemtrails”, mandatory vaccinations, and nanobots, all converging on “interactive orders with social engineering systems.” By August 2020 Kosol states the route plainly: “The chip has already been implemented through your flu-shots and through the Chemtrails, on purpose. And also through the food.”

The record also lets its interviewers push back. James Rink asks directly whether the Coronavirus is “being spread by Chemtrails?” — treating the trails as one suspect among several rather than an article of faith — and the answers keep the mechanisms distinct: the virus has its own vector; the trails belong to the chip program.

As throughout the archive: these are the record’s claims, preserved as spoken and verified by no one. What makes Project Chemtrail notable in the corpus is its specificity — a named project, named agencies, and a named purpose, stated in the very first session the community recorded.
