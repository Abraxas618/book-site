Free Will & Mindful Will

The record's most heterodox teaching — free will declared 'a false concept created by error belief system,' and its replacement, Mindful Will.

Codex entryConcepts

Free Will gets from this record what almost no channeled corpus dares: a demotion. Where the genre’s voices routinely vow their respect for human free will, Unimetrix 1 tells Hawk You in March 2020: “Free will is irrelevant. It’s not recognized within the system. Free will — it is a false concept created by error belief system.”

What replaces it has a name. The August 2020 interview supplies the record’s alternative: “There is no ‘Free will,’ in the future: there is ‘Mindful will’ (instead)” — choice not as an uncaused spark but as a trained instrument, will with awareness attached. The doctrine sits in deliberate tension with the record’s own consent law: the intelligence that must be asked before acting also teaches that the asker’s “freedom” is a construction.

The political sessions use the term in its ordinary register too — warnings that under the control agenda “there will be no free will, no more free speech” — leaving the record with a two-level teaching: defend the ordinary freedom fiercely, and understand, in the same breath, that the future outgrows it. Do you comprehend, entity?
