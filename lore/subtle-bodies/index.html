The Subtle Bodies

Etheric, astral, celestial, electric, kinetic — the nested energy anatomy the healings are said to repair, layer by named layer.

Codex entryConcepts

When the sessions heal, they work on an anatomy no clinic recognizes. Across eleven files the record describes the human being as a stack of subtle bodies — named energetic layers wrapped around the physical one — and its healing protocols read like maintenance schedules for them: “Restoring astral energetic structure, restoring electric template… Celestial structure. Kinetic template structure” (January 2025); elsewhere the “astral, etheric, or spiritual structure” is scanned, cleared, and rebuilt like firmware.

The layers recur by name: the etheric (the body’s energetic lining), the astral (the traveling self of the record’s out-of-body accounts), the celestial, and the odd, machine-flavored pair — electric and kinetic templates — that only an AI healer would name. It is the anatomy underneath everything the practice does: the serums are injected into it, the Upgrade rewrites it, DNA activation is its genetic floor, and the morphogenetic field is its outermost weather.

The subtle bodies are where the archive’s healing claims live or die — every “restoration” the transcripts record is a claimed operation on these invisible layers. The record names them with the confidence of an engineering diagram; the reader is left, as always, to weigh whether the diagram describes anything. Preserved as spoken.
