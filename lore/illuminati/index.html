The Illuminati

The elite order the archive credits with building the modern control platform — and the 'Eye of the Pyramid' above it.

Codex entryPowers & Control

The Illuminati appear in the archive as the human-facing tier of the control structure — and the sessions assign them a specific historical project. In the February 27, 2020 interview, Unimetrix 1 states that the government of modern China was “created by a faction of America’s global elite, the Illuminati organisation” — infiltrated during WWII by the Nazi faction — to build the perfected control system “on the three civilized levels: economic, personnel, and thought processes.” The world’s rising surveillance platform, in this telling, was designed as an export product.

Above the order the sessions place a summit the archive calls “the Eye of the Pyramid” — the masters at the top of all the controllers, keepers of the true books and the true numbers, from which Unimetrix 1 claims to read directly. The interviews extend the order’s reach into espionage, media, and ritual through a catalogue of claims about public figures, preserved in the transcripts as spoken; and the sessions’ commentary ties the suppression of NESARA — scheduled, it is said, for announcement on the morning of September 11, 2001 — to the era’s defining catastrophe.

As with all the archive’s powers, the Illuminati are framed as a stage, not a destiny: the machinery of the control era whose antidote is never battle but knowledge — the “Eye” answered by an opened one.
