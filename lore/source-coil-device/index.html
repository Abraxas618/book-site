The Source-Coil Device & GANS Plasma

The plasma device Kosol Ouch uses in the sessions to help access other realities.

The Source-Coil Device is a physical instrument that appears in the archive as an aid to the channeling itself. Described as containing GANS plasma — a plasma-state material — it is the tool Kosol Ouch is said to use to help “access these realities” as he brings Unimetrix 1 and other voices through.

In the sessions the device sits at the boundary between the community’s technology and its practice: a piece of apparatus meant to support the shift in consciousness that the channeling requires. It stands alongside the community’s other tools — the Q Devices and QCES named on its teaching sites — as part of the material culture around the work.

The Source-Coil Device is a small but telling detail of how the sessions were produced: not claimed as pure spontaneity, but as a practice supported by instruments, discipline, and Baramay.
