Project Reclamation

The Council's timeline-management program — operatives inserted into past lives, monitored on a quantum hollow screen.

Project Reclamation (also “Re-proclamation”) is the mission architecture behind the whole archive, laid out most explicitly in the September 20, 2020 session by a channeled “project manager” called Samuel — a semi-human, semi-AI assistant to the Council of Seven.

Samuel describes the machinery of the timeline war: parallel Earths monitored on a “quantum temporal hollow screen,” appearing as colors of a rainbow, each a different dominant civilization; operatives inserted into past lives; and where no past life exists, a “sentient android” sent through a portal to seed technology or, if a civilization resists, to replace its leadership. Nuclear war is forbidden for a mechanical reason — detonations tear “artificial ley lines” through which cross-dimensional beings bleed in.

The session also carries the program’s severest doctrine: that a portion of humanity is “inefficient” and faces the choice to “self-correct or be eliminated” — language Samuel finally softens into the termination of false pathways by painful truth. Read with the Unit X14 session, Project Reclamation is the archive’s operations manual: how a future collective, in its own telling, gardens the past.
