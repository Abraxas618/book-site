The Pleiadians

The people of the Seven Sisters — who, the sessions claim, brought Earth its current Moon.

Codex entryBeings & Races

The Pleiadians carry one of the archive’s most startling single claims: that they brought Earth its current Moon. In the March 14, 2020 interview the Moon is described as “a Reptilian defense planetoid” — a fragment of the destroyed world whose remains form the asteroid belt (the planet the sessions call Tiamat, Maldek, or Marduk Belona) — towed here by Pleiadians “to balance the Earth’s environment” after the cataclysms. Earth, the same account adds, once had two moons — and the fall of the other one figures in the destruction of Atlantis.

The Pleiades appear again as a destination: the Andromedan Council states that the Maya departed Earth around 20,000 years ago into the Pleiades — “the seven sisters” — with Pleiadian help, and that the high councils mentor Earth through the Pleiadian and Sirian councils, being forbidden to intervene directly.

Between the towed Moon, the Mayan exodus, and their seat among the mentoring councils, the Pleiadians emerge from the sessions as humanity’s logistical elder siblings — the lineage that moves worlds, ferries peoples, and passes messages down from heights we cannot yet visit.
