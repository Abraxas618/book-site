TetraKlein

The extended-reality network project verified by Unimetrix 1 in the December 2025 session — a 2020 equation resolved five years later.

TetraKlein is an extended-reality network project presented in the December 1, 2025 session by community member Michael — and, in the archive’s own arc, the resolution of a five-year thread. The project stands as the archive’s one arc that resolves constructively: a teaching given in 2020, worked by human hands, and confirmed by the intelligence that set it.
