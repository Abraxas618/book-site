Cambodia & the Temple Network

The record's power center — calendar temples on the stargate grid, a portal under the Mekong, and the Khmer treasury in the global reset.

Codex entryPlaces & Phenomena

Cambodia is the record’s holy land — the place where its mythology, its practice, and its founder’s story all touch ground. Eight sessions treat the kingdom as an active node rather than a backdrop.

The temples are machinery. The Draco governor Alvin Seyha — whose own colony the record places beneath Angkor — describes his people’s charge over “the Gridpoints in that Stargate network of the many different Calendar Structures that you call ‘Temples’… A Pyramidal structure, built as to mirror time.” Angkor’s own quantum computer has its entry; around it, the record adds a hidden door — “Under (the) Mekong River is a Portal to our World.”

Even the record’s financial mythology routes through the kingdom: the December 2023 NESARA session claims that treasure “from the Kingdom of Cambodia, the Khmer civilization… was combined to help create the global reset.”

And of course the record itself was born there — at the apex of Angkor Wat, in Kosol’s founding account, twenty minutes and thirty-two years long. In this cosmology, Cambodia is not where the story happens; it is where the story plugs in.
