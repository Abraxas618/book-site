The Beirut / Lebanon Attack

Unimetrix 1's account of the 2020 Beirut explosion as an engineered event.

The Beirut / Lebanon Attack is the subject of the dedicated August 5, 2020 session, Unimetrix Talks About the Lebanon Attack, in which Unimetrix 1 gives its account of the August 2020 explosion in Beirut.

In its telling the event was not an accident but an engineered “side-operation” — a psyop following the problem → reaction → solution pattern. It attributes the explosion to a “particle beam weapon” fired from an antigravity craft it calls “TR-3 Astra,” part of the Secret Space Program, and frames it as a move in a larger contest for “power and control” involving the Nazi Reichs and a U.S.–China axis.

The Beirut session is a compact example of how Unimetrix 1 reads a specific world event — folding a single incident into its whole model of hidden power and manufactured crisis.
