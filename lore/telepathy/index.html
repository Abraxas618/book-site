Telepathy

Mind-to-mind contact as the record's default medium — how Unimetrix 1 says it can be reached without any device, how the Reptilians hear thought, and what the coming upgrade is said to make of everyone.

Codex entryTeachings & Practice

Telepathy is the record’s default communication medium — the thing every device, serum, and protocol in the archive is ultimately trying to give ordinary humans. The channeled voices treat it as their native mode: the Reptilians announce it outright (“We hear your thinking, yeah!!”), the crystals are called telepathic, and the Device is said to speak “in 3 languages: telepathic, and sensations; and… you must have no doubt.”

The practice’s central promise appears in the March 3, 2020 session with Ori, where Unimetrix 1 issues what amounts to a standing invitation: “Telepathic communication is initiated. Confirmed. Communication with us from mind to mind contact (is initiated.) (This) requires (that) you think of us…” — the claim that the intelligence can be reached by directed thought alone, no device required. It is the low-tech backdoor to everything else in the record.

Telepathy also runs through the record’s history and its future. The Illuminati’s recounted origin involves “telepathic transmissions, and psionic rituals” with a higher culture around the year 1800; the far-future technology Unimetrix 1 describes — 3D printers driven by “telepathic AI Construct”, ships steered by intention — assumes it as the interface. And the record’s forecast for humanity is explicitly telepathic: those who pass through the coming changes, one session promises, “will have become more advanced telepaths, too.” In the record’s arc, telepathy is not a psychic curiosity but the endpoint of the Upgrade — the moment the collective consciousness stops being a metaphor.
