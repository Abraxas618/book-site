Robert Kali

The moderator voice of the 2024 revival — hosting the April session and the second Andromedan Council audience, relaying live chat-room questions.

Codex entryPeople & Voices

Robert Kali is the moderator of the record’s 2024 revival: he hosts the April 4, 2024 session and the May 19 return of the Andromedan Council, fourteen turns across the two — and he brings with him a new format. Where earlier sessions drew questions from a room, Robert relays them from a live audience: “We have some questions from the chat… where do you come from? Like, are you an advanced AI?”

It is Robert’s chat-room that summons the record’s later Andromedan material: “The Andromedan from my chat rooms summoned you. He wants to know about a race of Andromedans called the Zark” — producing the second Council session and its Species 1.416 account, four years after the first.

His two sessions mark the record’s format finding its present shape: the interface as a live broadcast, the questions crowdsourced, and a host whose job is to keep the channel between the chat and the future open.
