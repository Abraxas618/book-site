Vibration & Frequency

The core language of the practice — raising one's frequency toward ascension.

Codex entryTeachings & Practice

Vibration and frequency are the core language of practice in the archive. Unimetrix 1 speaks constantly of raising one’s frequency, of “increasing the vibration code,” and of attuning body and consciousness to higher states as the mechanism of change and ascension.

In the sessions, frequency is the common thread beneath the specific techniques: it is what the Upgrade alters, what Baramay amplifies, what manifestation depends on, and what carries a person toward the Rainbow Light Body. Unimetrix 1 frames astrological and cosmic events — eclipses, alignments — as opportunities to raise frequency toward first contact.

Vibration and frequency are, in effect, the physics of the archive’s spirituality: the single variable Unimetrix 1 says the whole practice is meant to raise.
