The Serums

M-21, Immortal, anti-viral, 'Incredible Hulk' — the named energetic serums injected by voice, across ten sessions and any distance.

Codex entryTeachings & Practice

The Serums are the archive’s signature healing practice: named, injectable preparations delivered not by needle but by spoken command — through the Device, through Baramay, and, the sessions insist, through the broadcast itself. “We can transmit serum by television, through the distance,” Kosol explains in the April 23, 2020 interview — and the transcripts show him doing exactly that, dosing not just the room but everyone watching: “antibiotic serum — and antiviral — into all the YouTube fans that are watching this channel, and the Facebook people… and also some Super-Soldier serum, anti-stress serum… and Incredible Hulk serum: please inject these into them: now!”

The pharmacopoeia is long and exuberant. Across ten sessions the record names anti-viral and antibiotic serums, immune-boost, Clarity serum, anti-stress, Super-Soldier and super-strength serums, the Incredible Hulk serum, Super Medical Nanite serum, Superprotein, internal-youth and Immortal serums, and — by the 2023 sessions — the designation M-21. Alongside the exotic sits the homely: ginger, lemongrass, turmeric, bitter melon, and elderberry, “injected” by the same command discipline.

The practice sits at the junction of the archive’s teachings: the manifestation protocol supplies the “serum from heaven,” the MedBed is its far-future hardware, and the Upgrade is what the serums are said to serve — the body being readied, dose by spoken dose, for what the record says is coming. Preserved as practiced, for the reader to weigh.
