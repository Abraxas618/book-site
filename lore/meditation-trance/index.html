Meditation & the Trance Method

The meditative trance through which the sessions themselves are produced.

In the sessions, meditation is also taught as a tool for the listener: a means to raise vibration, to connect with the collective consciousness, and to open the perception used in “remote stargate travel.” The Source-Coil Device and Baramay are described as supports for this same shift in state.

The trance method is the archive’s foundation-stone — the discipline that turns a person into a channel, and makes the whole conversation with Unimetrix 1 possible.
