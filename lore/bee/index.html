Bee

The most persistent questioner of the autumn-2020 channeled-guest cycle — nine sessions from the Anunnaki to the political clones.

Codex entryPeople & Voices

Bee is the great questioner of the record’s autumn 2020 — present in nine sessions, greeting each visiting intelligence with the same warm formality: “Hi, teacher, this is Bee. How are you?” Unimetrix 1 returns the courtesy in kind: “Greeting entity Bee.”

Her questions draw out some of the record’s most memorable material. To the Anunnaki teacher: “Hello, Father Anu… My question is, how were humans created and at what time were we created?” — prompting the creation-story session of December 2020. And it is Bee’s question about political candidates in the October 11, 2020 session that produces the record’s bluntest claim about cloning: that much of the political class is “biological copy or clones” — the exchange cut short by interference moments after she asks for names.

Across the Insectoid, Andromedan, and Anunnaki audiences, Bee is the community’s gentle interrogator — the voice that says thank you to every teacher, and keeps asking anyway.
