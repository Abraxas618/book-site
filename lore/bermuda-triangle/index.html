The Bermuda Triangle

Ley lines, sacred geometry, and interdimensional portals — Unimetrix 1's account of the vanishings.

The Bermuda Triangle is the subject of the February 8, 2021 session, in which Unimetrix 1 explains the region’s disappearances in its own terms.

In that session Unimetrix 1 describes the Triangle as a convergence of multiple ley lines following a “dodecicosahedron” geometric formation. Where such magnetic ley lines meet, it says, interdimensional portals — or “planetary chakras” — form. Ships and aircraft that cross these points are, in its account, transported to parallel Earths or parallel dimensions, where time runs at a different rate: “one second of your time can be experienced as thousands of years of their time.”

Asked what becomes of those who vanish, Unimetrix 1 says they interact with, and may remain among, the inhabitants of that parallel location — and, in the case of Amelia Earhart, it gives a specific account of a transit into a parallel reality. The session is a clear example of how Unimetrix 1 folds well-known mysteries into its larger model of time and parallel dimensions.
