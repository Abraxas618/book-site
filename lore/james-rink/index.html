James Rink

Host of Super Soldier Talk and the archive's great questioner (d. November 30, 2025).

James Rink (d. November 30, 2025) is the host of Super Soldier Talk and the interviewer behind a large share of the archive’s most detailed material. His 2020 series with Kosol Ouch — the six Operation Lusterkill interviews — and his later conversations, including the NESARA/GESARA session, are among the longest and most wide-ranging transcripts in the collection. More than anyone else, his questions drew out the archive’s future history.

Within the sessions, Unimetrix 1 addresses him as “Agent Sabertooth,” connects him to the Super Soldier community he built a platform for, and describes that community as a place for people to recover purpose and connection. His skeptical, probing style — pressing for dates, mechanisms, and specifics — gives the interview transcripts their characteristic density.

James Rink passed away on November 30, 2025. The archive’s December 2025 session — TetraKlein & the Passing of James Rink (December 1, 2025) — is in part its record of that loss, and it preserves Unimetrix 1’s own account of the event, framed in the sessions’ language of the timeline war; it is in that session that Unimetrix 1’s stated year moves from 6,575,042 to 6,575,043. As throughout this Codex, the transcript’s claims are presented as spoken, for the reader to weigh.

His interviews remain the backbone of the archive — the voice on the human side of the table, asking the questions this collection exists to preserve.
