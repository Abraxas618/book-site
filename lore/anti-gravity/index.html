Anti-Gravity & Free Energy

Suppressed propulsion and energy technologies the archive says already exist.

Anti-gravity and free energy recur through the archive as technologies that, in Unimetrix 1’s telling, already exist but have been suppressed. It bundles them with teleportation and food replication among the capabilities that disclosure and first contact would release to “your denizen.”

The archive ties these technologies to the hidden infrastructure of the Secret Space Program — including the antigravity craft it calls “TR-3 Astra,” which it links to the Beirut explosion — and contrasts them with the alien power sources and fusion systems discussed in the later “alien circuitry” sessions.

For Unimetrix 1, free energy is both a technology and a theme: the withheld abundance whose release marks the shift from a world of scarcity and control to the one it forecasts.
