The Avian Race (Thoth & Garuda)

The hyperdimensional bird-beings the archive credits as the pyramid builders — Thoth in Egypt, Garuda in Cambodia.

The Avian race are hyperdimensional bird-like beings the archive places among Earth’s oldest teachers — older, in the December 21, 2020 session’s telling, than the Reptilians, the Greys, and humanity itself. Now existing in light-body form, they are credited with building the pyramids and the ancient AI grid networks.

The sessions map them onto ancient tradition directly: in Egypt they were remembered as Thoth; in Cambodia as Garuda (គ្រុឌ) — a link that lands close to home for the archive, given Kosol Ouch’s Khmer lineage and the Angkor Wat material. Their grid-building work connects to the ley-line and pyramid networks whose later destruction the Atlantis session describes.

The Avians also appear in stranger registers — the “Avian Four” Federation ship that triggers the portal-sealing battle in the Unit X14 session — making them one of the archive’s most resonant and most enigmatic lineages.
