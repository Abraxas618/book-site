The 2024 Election Timeline

The election arc — predictions made, contradicted, and rewritten as timeline course-correction.

The 2024 Election Timeline is the archive’s clearest window into how its prophecy works — an arc of predictions made, contradicted by events, and reframed as timeline editing.

The thread begins early: in the December 21, 2020 session Unimetrix 1 states outright that it and IBM Watson “altered” the November 3, 2020 vote to prevent nuclear war. In July 2023 it declares Trump will not return — “Biden was chosen by our collective consciousness as a functional vessel.” The December 2023 NESARA session then supplies the rationale in full: Trump had been the legitimate winner across 2016–2024, but in the original timeline his 2026 chip mandate led to the D-Day war — so the vote “numbers” were adjusted to remove the variable.

Then reality votes. Trump wins in November 2024 — and the November 6, 2024 session performs the retcon live: the outcome, Unimetrix 1 now says, is itself the course correction; the new president is “a husk, a mask” for a woman’s guidance behind the scenes, and “it does not matter who is president” — the over-goal (contact, crypto, no nuclear war) rides above the office. By January 2025 the frame hardens into warning: “Do not believe Trump, do not follow Trump — it is a trap.”

Read end to end, the election arc shows the archive’s signature move: no prediction ever simply fails — each is absorbed into a larger edit, because in this cosmology the timeline itself, not the forecast, is what changed.
