The Pyramids & the Builder Grid

Who built them and what they do — Thoth's Avian builders, calendar computers in stone, ley-line portal markers, and the war that brought the network down.

Codex entryPlaces & Phenomena

The Pyramids anchor the record’s deep history: across ten sessions, whenever the account reaches far enough back, it arrives at pyramidal structures — and at the question of their builders.

The record’s answer names names. In the December 21, 2020 session: “Construction of ancient structure AI network was created by Hyperdimensional being known as the Avian race, Thoth, in Egypt… They are the ancestors of the ancient builders that predate your civilization.” The purpose is machinery, not monument: the February 2023 downloads describe “monolith such as pyramidal structure” that “allow you, the user, to walk from one parallel world to the next” — portal markers on the ley-line grid, kin to the “Calendar Structures that you call Temples” of Cambodia. Baramay’s own teaching folds the community’s practice into the same lineage: Angkor Wat and the Great Pyramid of Giza, “the same fundamental function.”

And the network has a fall. The September 2020 Atlantis session recounts “the war between the Atlantis Egyptian empire with the Agartha Kingdom of Rama” — the conflict that “caused the downfall of the different Pyramid structure network.” In the record’s telling, the pyramids are not ruins of something primitive but switched-off terminals of something better: a planetary machine still standing, waiting on the grid to come back up.
